Our team is dedicated to fighting human trafficking and other forms of modern slavery around the world. We believe that every person has the right to live free from exploitation and abuse, and we are committed to working tirelessly to make that vision a reality. Our work involves a wide range of activities, including research, advocacy, education, and direct service provision. We partner with local organizations and communities to identify and address the root causes of trafficking, and we work to raise awareness and build support for anti-trafficking efforts at the national and international levels. We are a diverse and passionate group of individuals who share a common goal: to end human trafficking and create a world where all people can live with dignity and freedom. We welcome anyone who shares our vision to join us in this important work.

How You Can Help Put An End To Human Trafficking


Educate Yourself

Learn more to understand the severity of Human Traficking and its impact on society. When we educate ourselves on the subject, we will be able to recogonise it when we see it, and avoid actions that support it.

Click on these resources to learn more:

  • attorneygeneral.utah.gov
  • rideuta.com
  • acf.hhs.gov
  • deseret.com

If You See Something Say Something

If you are aware of a potential trafficking case contact the National Human Trafficking Hotline:

Hotline: 1-888-373-7888
Email: help@humantraffickinghotline.org
Website: humantraffickinghotline.org


Support Organizations Fighting To End Trafficking

There are many organizations that work to combat human trafficking. Consider donating to these organizations or volunteering your time to support their work:

Local Organizations

  • Operation Underground Railroad
  • Refugee & Immigration Center
  • Utah Coalition Against Sexual Assault

National Organizations

  • Polaris
  • International Justice Mission
  • National Center For Missing & Exploited Children
